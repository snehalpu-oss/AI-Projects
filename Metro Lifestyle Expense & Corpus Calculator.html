Corpus Building Plan

How corpus is calculated: Corpus = Present Value of all future inflation-adjusted annual expenses over 5 years, discounted at your expected investment return. SIP = fixed monthly investment to accumulate that corpus in 60 months. Rent uses its own inflation rate; EMIs are treated as flat. Figures are indicative — consult a financial advisor for personalised planning.
